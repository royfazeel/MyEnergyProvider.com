Frequently Asked Questions

How does your service work?

Simply call (888) 524-0250, provide your energy provider and account information, and our specialist will process your payment securely. You'll receive instant confirmation.

Which energy providers do you support?

We support over 50 major energy providers including Duke Energy, PG&E, Southern California Edison, FPL, Dominion Energy, Con Edison, Xcel Energy, and many more.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely! We use bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption and are PCI-DSS compliant. Your payment information is never stored and is processed through secure payment networks.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, and ACH bank transfers.

Are you affiliated with energy providers?

No, MyEnergyProvider is an independent third-party bill payment service. We are NOT affiliated with any energy provider mentioned on this website.

What are your hours of operation?

We are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. You can call us anytime to make a payment.

Is there a fee for using your service?

A small convenience fee may apply. Our representative will inform you of any fees before processing your payment, so there are no surprises.
