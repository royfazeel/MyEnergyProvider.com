Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

When you use our service, we collect information necessary to process your payment including your name, contact information, energy provider account number, and payment details.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to process your energy bill payment, provide customer support, send payment confirmations, and improve our services.

Data Security

All payment information is encrypted using 256-bit SSL encryption. We are PCI-DSS compliant and do not store your complete payment card information after the transaction is processed.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We only share information with payment processors as necessary to complete your transaction.
